The main motivations for users on UGl.st

- Connect
  People who are trying to connect with the leadership and members of the User-Group communities.

- Build
  People who organize User-Group meetings.

- Participate
  People who attend User-Group meetings.


Builders log in and register an account.
Builders create User-Group profiles.
Builders enter payment information.
Builders receive funds.
Builders update sponsorship obligations.
Builders manage their User-Group profiles.
Builders promote meetings for their user-groups.


Participants login and register an account.
Particpants manage their profile.
Participants join and leave User-Groups.
Participants leave messages for UG organizers and other participants
Participants participants receive responses from UG organizers and other participants
Participants embed media for User-Group meetings.
